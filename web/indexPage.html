Defence
Thales helps to equip almost every major platform in service or in development with the armed forces. We have extensive expertise in intelligence, surveillance, target acquisition and reconnaissance (ISTAR). Our deep technological understanding and strong electronic systems integration track record has seen us develop and perfect a leading portfolio of systems, products and services for the export market. Through life system support for customers on a global basis, including operational theatres, is another key strength. While our capability in military training/simulation – enables forces to reduce costs.

Security Overview
We work closely with our customers to define the future needs of homeland security and the protection of critical national infrastructure using technologies such as secure communication systems, encryption technology, command and control systems and chemical, biological, radiological and nuclear solutions.

Transportation
As a pioneer in transportation technology, we are one of the world’s top suppliers of systems, solutions, equipment and services for mobility on railways, roads and public networks. We help our customers operate infrastructures at higher capacities so passengers and goods can reach their destinations more quickly, more safely, more ecologically and at a lower cost.
